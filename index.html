Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about SunPump

What is SunPump?

SunPump is an all-in-one platform for creating meme tokens, adding liquidity, and using MEV sniper bots on the TRON blockchain. It's designed to make token creation and advanced trading accessible to everyone.

How does the MEV Sniper work?

Our MEV Sniper monitors the TRON mempool for new token launches and liquidity additions. When it detects an opportunity, it automatically executes trades with optimized gas settings to front-run other traders, maximizing your profit potential.

Is SunPump safe to use?

Yes, SunPump's smart contracts have been audited by leading security firms. As a non-custodial platform, you maintain control of your funds at all times. We also include rug-pull detection features to help identify potentially malicious tokens.

What are the fees?

Token creation has a fixed fee of 100 TRX. MEV Sniper usage requires a subscription of 500 TRX per month or 5,000 TRX annually. There are no percentage fees on transactions beyond standard network gas costs.
